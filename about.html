About Me

Hey there, I’m Masani Santhosh Kumar — someone who always thinks “what if?” and enjoys working toward the answer.

My Journey

I’m currently pursuing my B.Tech second year in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I didn’t choose this branch because I was passionate about it at the beginning, but because I never wanted to be in the crowd.

Over time, this branch slowly pulled me in and woke up my inner kid. Electronics started explaining things I had seen since childhood — how much work happens behind even the smallest action.

Something as simple as changing a TV channel once felt instant, but ECE taught me how much processing, signaling, and control happens behind it.

How I Think

I don’t like doing random projects just for the sake of it. I want my work to have real-world relevance and meaning.

I strongly believe that knowing the basics and then learning while working is better than waiting to learn everything before starting. My mistakes teach me more than theory ever could.
