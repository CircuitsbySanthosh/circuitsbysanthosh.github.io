About Me

Who I Am

I’m Masani Santhosh Kumar, currently pursuing B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I’m someone who is curious about how systems actually work behind the scenes — not just in theory, but in real hardware.

What started as just a branch choice slowly turned into genuine interest. Working with circuits, microcontrollers, and sensors made me realize how much engineering goes into even the simplest everyday actions.

How I Approach Engineering

I believe in learning by building. Instead of waiting to master every concept, I start with strong fundamentals and improve through experimentation, debugging, and iteration.

Most of my understanding has come from building systems, making mistakes, and refining them until they work reliably.

What I Work On

My work focuses on practical electronics systems using embedded platforms like Arduino. I’m especially interested in sensor-based systems, signal processing basics, and designing low-cost hardware solutions that can solve real-world problems.
